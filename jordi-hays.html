Pitch Decks

The highest form of storytelling

“Don Valentine talks about how capital follows stories. A deck is a form of storytelling. In fact, I think it’s the highest form of storytelling there is. Condensing a very complex idea or business into 20 slides is not easy. I’ve spent countless nights in Figma over the last year just helping my portfolio companies make decks. If you’re a company planning to raise any amount of money or selling any products at all you gotta get good at this game. You gotta have someone on your team that’s truly cracked at it” Jordi (Dec 11, 2024)

Make pitch decks with your guy, not AI

“I’ve tried a lot of the AI decks and there’s nothing like the dynamic of working with a fantastic deck designer. That’s like saying, if you’re driving a rally car ‘I don’t want my co-pilot, I’ll just use an AI.’ It’s like you want your guy co-pilot making the deck with you and you can make much more interesting materials” Jordi (Dec 11, 2024)

Virality

Manufacturing Virality

“I think that the thing that we were aware of is that a lot of people have this idea that virality on the Internet is sort of random, you can't manufacture it, etcetera. I think that's totally false. When you have a subculture like tech, Twitter and the startup community that we're a part of, it's actually completely possible to manufacture virality, where almost every single thing that we've done on the drops side of the marketing team we knew was going to go viral [Party Round/Capital], because, we were the audience for it. I think if you really understand a subculture, it's a lot easier to generate awareness in that subculture” Jordi (Dec 2021)

Narrative Market Fit

“Does a company have product-market-fit or does a company have narrative-market-fit? You actually get crazier multiples for narrative-market-fit” Jordi (Oct 14, 2024)

Utility & Joy

Utility + Joy = Best products

“One of the ideas that comes up over and over again is how do you create something with a lot of utility that also creates a lot of joy? Historically, those two things were almost at odds. A product either was joyful or had a ton of utility. And the best products today find ways to combine those two things in a really magic way” Jordi (Dec 2021)

Strategy

“Oftentimes, startups are not about being the most innovative around the core product or creating something completely novel, or designing a process or experience that never existed before. It’s simply about looking at existing behavior in the marketplace and then building a product that leans into all of that existing activity” Jordi (Dec 2021)
